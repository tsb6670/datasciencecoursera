CodeBook

Data set is stored in: course3_wk4_data/GettingandCleaningDataCourseProject.csv

Variables are:

"" - first column, empty header - this is the row number

subject_id - this is the subject identifier

activity_id - this is the activity identifier

activity_name - this is the descriptive name for the activity identifier (activity_id)

type - identifies the class of variable labeled as type

mean - the mean corresponding to the collected measurement (one measurement per row)

std - the standard deviation corresponding to the collected measurement (one measurement per row)

avg_mean - the average mean corresponding to the class variable (average of all measurements of type/class)

avg_std - the average standard corresponding to the class variable (average of all measurements of type/class)
